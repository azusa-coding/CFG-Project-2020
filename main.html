About Us


Welcome!

We are Giulia and Azusa, two international students studying in Edinburgh! Despite coming from different countries - Italy and Japan - and studying for different majors - Linguistics and Physics-, there is a big passion we have in common: cooking! In fact, at times, there is nothing better that can remind us of home, than our favourite dish, especially if shared with someone! We are thus looking forward to share with all of you our favourite recipes and guide you through them in an easy and quick way. Enjoy!

Giulia and Azusa
