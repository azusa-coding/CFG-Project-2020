Miso Soup


15 min

Miso soup is a traditional and most common soup in Japan. We enjoy this soup almost every with different ingredients. Here are the recipes with the Tofu, seaweed and leek which are used most commonly but you can add anything you like! Hope you will like it!

Ingredients

  • 3 tsp Miso paste
  • 350ml water
  • 1 leek
  • 1 tsp dashi stock
  • 1 block Tofu
  • seaweed

Procedure

  1. Pour and heat water and the dashi stock to an empty pot.
  2. Cut Tofu and leek into small dice.
  3. Add the Tofu, leek and seaweed cook for 5 min.
  4. Turn OFF the heat and add Miso paste and mix well.Serve immediately.
